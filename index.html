4th Of July Tournament

Organized by RCS

Tournament Details

  • Date: July 5, 2025
  • Location: Goodrich Park
  • Format: Double Elimination
  • Team Selection: Draft

About the Tournament

This is a fun and supportive tournament where teams will compete in a double elimination format. The teams will be selected via a draft, ensuring a competitive and exciting tournament.

The tournament is organized by RCS and is completely free to participate in.
